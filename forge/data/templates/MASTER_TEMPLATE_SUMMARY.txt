KEY PARAMETERS FOR LLM DRIVERS
================================================================================

1. cathode.loading_mg_cm2
   - Default: 19.674 mg/cm²
   - Range: [5.0 - 30.0]
   - Trade-off: ↑ loading = ↑ energy, ↓ power, ↓ cycle life
   - Coupled with: anode.loading_mg_cm2 (N/P ratio)

2. cathode.material_name
   - Default: NCM (Ni-90.5)
   - Options: NCM811, NCA, LFP, LCO
   - Trade-off: NCA = higher energy but lower life; LFP = safer

3. anode.loading_mg_cm2
   - Default: 11.478 mg/cm²
   - Range: [5.0 - 20.0]
   - Constraint: N/P > 1.05 (prevent lithium plating)

4. stack_config.electrode_pairs_per_stack
   - Default: 44 pairs
   - Trade-off: More pairs = more capacity, thicker stack

5. envelope.external.*
   - Determines physical cell size
   - cell_height_mm: [50-200] mm
   - cell_width_mm: [100-400] mm
   - cell_thickness_mm: [10-80] mm

6. electrolyte.excess_factor
   - Default: 1.0
   - Trade-off: Higher = better safety but more weight
