# CellCAD Engine

The engine layer (orge.engine) is a deterministic parametric modeling tool for lithium-ion battery cells supporting pouch, prismatic, and cylindrical form factors.

*Detailed engine documentation will be expanded from the project's internal design documents.*

## Capabilities

- Parametric geometry generation (electrode stacks, jellyrolls, housings)
- Mass and energy density calculations
- Bill of Materials (BOM) generation
- Cost estimation
- Schema and physics constraint validation
- CAD export (STEP, STL, FreeCAD script)
- 25 validated commercial cell archetypes
